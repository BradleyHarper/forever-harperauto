About

My name is Bradley Harper and I'm a professional small engine mechanic offering on-site small engine repair. I am a former A.S.E certified automotive mechanic with a degree in automotive technology and a passion for small engines. My goal is to have you fully aware of the repair process, perform a quality repair, and provide a same-day diagnosis, quick, and reliable repair. Proper waste disposal included for free.

My Goal with HarperAuto

While working as a mechanic on automobiles and small engine appliances the biggest issue I noticed was the shop turn-around time. Possibly several weeks without your equipment. On top of that, you need to haul or pay someone to bring the vehicle/appliance to the shop location. These parts of the repair process are removed while costing less than a traditional shop repair fee.

Repair Types

I repair any single cylinder engine that starts with a key, pull cord, and/or kickstart.
