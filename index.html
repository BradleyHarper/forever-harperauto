About Harper Auto

Hello! My name is Bradley Harper and I'm a professional small engine mechanic offering on-site small engine repair. My goal is to have you fully aware of the repair process, perform a quality repair, and provide a same-day diagnosis.

Equipment

I repair anything with a pull cord. From brands ranging from Toro, Stihl, Redmax, Briggs and Stratton, Honda, Craftsman, and more.

Pricing

On booking you\'ll see my rate per hour(s). If the job only takes an hour, you pay an hour. The final bill will have the cost of parts included.

Repair Types

Hard to start hand held equipment / push mower wearing out your shoulder? Self propelled drive on push mower not working anymore? I'm also open to helping to help finish a project, maybe we could both learn a thing or two!

Location
